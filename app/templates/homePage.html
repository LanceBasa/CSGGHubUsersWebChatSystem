Welcome to CSGGHUB!!

This is a CITS3403 - Agile Web Development Project in Collaboration with Lance, Sahil and Midhin.

Users will be able to create accounts by providing their desired username, email address, and password. This information will be stored securely in the system's back-end database. Registered users can log in to the system using their credentials. This process will authenticate the user's identity and grant them access to the chat system. The chat system will allow users to communicate with each other in real-time. They will be able to send text messages. The chat history will be logged in the back-end database, ensuring that conversations are retained for future reference. The back-end database will store information about registered users, including their usernames, email addresses, and hashed passwords. This data will be used for user authentication during the login process. Users will have the ability to chat with each other, form teams, and find suitable teammates for playing CSGO. They can discuss strategies, share their gaming preferences, and coordinate to create a perfect team that can rule the game!
